OutreachAI was founded in 2023 by Joshua Jenkinson, a Google Cloud engineer and software developer with a background in computer science and community support. Joshua was inspired by the potential of artificial intelligence to transform the world and make it a better place, particularly in improving communication and helping people connect more effectively.
He founded OutreachAI with the aim of using AI technology to help businesses and organizations of all sizes improve their outreach efforts and achieve their goals. Joshua assembled a team of experts in AI, machine learning, and data analytics to work tirelessly on developing cutting-edge products and services that are flexible, powerful, and easy to use.
While OutreachAI is still in beta, the team is working hard to refine their products and services and provide their clients with the best possible experience. With Joshua's vision and his team's hard work, OutreachAI quickly became a leading provider of AI-powered outreach solutions, serving clients in a wide range of industries.
